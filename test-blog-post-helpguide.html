Getting Started

Logging In

  1. Go to epictranscriptions.com and enter your username and password.
  2. Enter the verification code sent to your registered phone or email (2-factor authentication).
  3. Click Verify & Sign In to access your Client Portal.

Your Account

Your account is set up by EPIC Translations and is linked to your organization. Depending on your plan, you may have one or multiple users on your account. Each user has their own login credentials.

Tip: If you need additional users added to your account, contact your EPIC account manager.

Dashboard Overview

Your Client Portal dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of your translation and interpreting projects:

  • Active Projects - Projects currently being worked on by EPIC's linguists.
  • Completed - Finished projects ready for your review.
  • Pending Quotes - Quote requests awaiting EPIC's response.

Quick Actions

  • Submit Project - Start a new translation or interpreting request.
  • Request Quote - Get a cost estimate before committing.
  • My Projects - View all your projects and their statuses.

Submitting a Project

To submit a new translation or interpreting project:

  1. Click Submit Project in the sidebar or dashboard.
  2. Select the project type (Document Translation, Interpreting OPI, Interpreting VRI, On-Site Interpreting, etc.).
  3. Choose the source and target languages.
  4. Enter the project description and any special instructions for the linguist.
  5. Set the deadline for when you need the deliverable.
  6. Upload source files (for translation projects - supported formats: DOCX, PDF, PPTX, XLSX).
  7. Click Submit to send the project to EPIC for processing.

What Happens Next

After submission, EPIC's project management team will:

  • Review your project requirements
  • Assign the most qualified linguist based on language pair and expertise
  • Send you a confirmation with the assigned linguist and timeline
  • Begin work on your project
Tip: The more detail you provide in the project instructions, the better the result. Include context about the audience, tone, and any preferred terminology.

Tracking Your Projects

Go to My Projects in the sidebar to see all your projects and their current status.

Project Statuses

  • Available - Project received, awaiting linguist assignment.
  • Assigned - A linguist has been assigned and work is in progress.
  • Completed - The linguist has finished. Your deliverable is ready.
  • Delivered - The final files have been sent to you.
  • Revision - You've requested changes and the linguist is revising.
  • Invoiced - An invoice has been generated for this project.

Downloading Deliverables

Once a project is marked as Completed or Delivered, you can download the translated files directly from the project details page in EPS.
